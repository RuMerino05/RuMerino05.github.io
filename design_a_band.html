While waiting in line for bacon pancakes, Holly saw that Jake had drumsticks in his back pocket and went up to him and asked, “Hey i'm Holly, um I just wanted to ask a quick question, do you play in a band ? ” Jake responded “No I don’t I play drum in my basement” Holly immediately asked Jake if he wanted to start a band with him. Jake agreed on joining and told Holly that he knew some people who would also join if he asked them. Holly was pleased by this and gave Jake his information. Later on that week Jake called Holly telling her that his 2 friends would love to join the band. The next day they all got together and decided that their genre was going to be would be rock like Interpol.
